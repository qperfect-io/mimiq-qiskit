mimiq-qiskit
============

Qiskit ``BackendV2`` bridge for MIMIQ. Build circuits with
``qiskit.QuantumCircuit``, submit them to the MIMIQ cloud (or any local
MIMIQ simulator), and receive a familiar ``qiskit.result.Result``.
